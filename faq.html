FAQ


What is Pomfe?

Pomfe is a simple file hosting service. It lets you share your photos, documents, videos, or any other type of file completely anonymously.

What kind content is allowed to be uploaded?

You may upload any type of content as long as it does not infringe the laws of the European Union and/or Lithuania.

Do you keep any logs of uploaded content or users?

Pomfe does not keep records of any kind.

How do you handle content takedowns?

Pomfe.co respects takedowns for illegal or copyrighted content. Send a e-mail to votton@nyanpasu.tv (DMCA or Court Order)
