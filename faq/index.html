FAQ

What is Pomfe?

Pomfe is a simple file hosting service. It lets you share your photos, documents, videos or any other type of file completely anonymously.

What kind of content is allowed to be uploaded?

You may upload any type of content as long as it does not infringe the laws of the EU and/or France.

Do you keep any logs of uploaded content or users?

Pomfe does not keep records of any kind.

How do you handle content takedowns?

Pomfe.co respects takedowns for illegal or copyrighted content. You may send an e-mail to votton(at)auroware.com with a DMCA or court order.
